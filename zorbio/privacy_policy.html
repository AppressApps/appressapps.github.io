Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

Zorbio ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you play our mobile game, Zorbio.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in privacy by design. Zorbio is designed to minimize data collection:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous gameplay data (e.g., high scores, levels reached, crash reports) to help us improve the game performance and balance.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic information about your device, such as the model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to ensure compatibility and support.
  • Advertising IDs: To provide free gameplay, we use third-party advertising services (e.g., Google AdMob). These services may collect your device's advertising ID to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the game.
  • To improve and optimize the gameplay experience.
  • To monitor for technical issues and bugs.
  • To serve relevant advertisements through third-party partners.

3. Third-Party Services

The game uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is designed for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
